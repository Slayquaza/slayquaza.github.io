Commission Guidelines

1. Contact me with your idea and any references.

2. We'll discuss the commission details and make sure we're on the same page.

3. I'll create a sketch for your approval.

4. Once the sketch has been approved, payment is due (unless otherwise agreed).

5. I'll begin rendering the artwork and keep you updated throughout the process.

6. The finished artwork will be delivered digitally in full resolution.

Visual references are highly encouraged. They don't have to be a single, complete reference sheet—multiple images are perfectly fine. Feel free to combine references for hairstyles, outfits, colors, poses, accessories, expressions, or anything else that helps communicate your idea.

The more visual information you provide, the better I can bring your vision to life.

You'll receive a sketch for approval before rendering begins.

Reasonable revisions are included during the sketch stage.

Once the sketch has been approved, only minor revisions are included. Significant changes after rendering has begun may require an additional fee.

Payment is due after the sketch has been approved, unless otherwise agreed.

Payment methods will be added here once they have been finalized.

  • I reserve the right to decline any commission for any reason.
  • Clear communication and visual references are highly encouraged throughout the commission process.
  • All artwork will be created in my own style.
  • Commercial usage must be discussed and agreed upon before work begins. Additional licensing fees may apply.
  • Work on the final rendering will begin once payment has been received.
  • I retain the copyright to all commissioned artwork.
  • I may display commissioned artwork in my portfolio and on social media unless requested otherwise.
  • You may share the artwork online for personal use, non-commercial use. Credit is appreciated whenever possible.
  • Commercial usage must be discussed and agreed upon before work begins.
  • The artwork may not be used for AI training, NFTs, or resold without my explicit permission.
